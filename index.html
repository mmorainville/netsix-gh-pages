Features

With Netsix, sharing videos with your friends is fast and simple.
Once connected in peer-to-peer, you can stream and watch videos stored on a friend's hard drive as fast as possible.
Download Netsix.

Fast

Netsix uses WebRTC to create a real peer-to-peer connection.

Simple

Installing and using Netsix is really simple: just download the app and share your ID.

Secure

The WebRTC connexion is naturally encrypted and no one can see what you are sharing with your friends.

Open Source

The code is on GitHub and you are free to help improve the app.

Getting started

Watch videos from your friends in 6 easy steps !

  • Step 1

    Get your ID

    Open the app and click on "Copy" in the upper right corner.

  • Step 2

    Share your ID

    Share the ID you just copied with a friend who want to watch your videos.

  • Step 3

    Connect

    Enter your friend's ID in the "Remote" tab.

  • Step 4

    Choose your video

    Choose a video from your friend's collection in the "Collections" panel.

  • Step 5

    Wait a short time

    Your friend is automatically preparing the video for you before streaming it.

  • Step 6
    Enjoy
    Your video
